Hi, I'm Konstantine, a student who decided to take control of my health after struggling with balancing school, stress, and living a healthy lifestyle.

Like many, I found it hard to figure out where to start, with so much conflicting advice online. But after realizing how important it was to feel good both mentally and physically, I decided to make some changes.

Through trial and error, I found simple, sustainable habits that made a real difference. Now, I’ve created FitGuide to help others who are in the same position I was. Whether you're juggling school, work, or just life in general, my goal is to share practical tips and resources to help you live a healthier, happier life.

Values and Mission

At FitGuide, my mission is to make healthy living accessible and achievable for everyone. I believe in simplicity, balance, and consistency—because small, positive changes lead to long-term results.

My values are rooted in empowerment, education, and community. I aim to provide practical advice, support, and resources that help individuals take charge of their health and well-being, no matter where they’re starting from.

I am here to inspire and guide you on your journey to a healthier lifestyle, one step at a time.
